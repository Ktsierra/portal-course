Welcome — Your new JJA Aviation device

Use the Intune Company Portal to install work apps and finish setup. It's pinned to the taskbar next to Outlook (Mail).

What is the Intune Company Portal?
The Intune Company Portal is JJA Aviation's app portal (like a company app store). Use it to find, install and get updates for approved work apps. IT uses it to keep devices secure and apps up to date.
All company apps are installed through Company Portal. If you need software for work, open Company Portal first — do not install from other sites.
Where is it on your device?
The Company Portal is pinned to the taskbar by default. Look for the briefcase/shield icon next to the Outlook (Mail) icon on the taskbar. Click it to open Company Portal quickly.
If you don't see it on the taskbar, open the Start menu and type Company Portal to find and pin it.
How to get apps (quick)
1. Open Company Portal (taskbar or Start). 2. Go to the Apps or Available apps section. 3. Select the app, read its description, then click Install or Get.
Installation runs in the background. Wait for it to finish — apps may appear in Start or on your desktop when ready.
Always read the app description
App pages include a short description, publisher, permissions and any special steps you must follow. Read these instructions carefully before you use the app.
Example above: this is an app page in Company Portal — click the image to view it fullscreen and read the description for required steps or sign-in instructions.
Important: Special app instructions
Some apps require extra steps in their description to work correctly. Two important examples for JJA Aviation:
  1. 3CX Phone App: Follow the description steps exactly to configure the phone app (provisioning, permissions, or sign-in). Without these steps the phone app will not work.
  2. Adobe Creative Cloud / Acrobat: Follow the app description for sign-in and activation steps if you need Adobe Acrobat or Creative Cloud tools.
If a description tells you to sign in, grant permissions, or restart — follow those steps. Ask IT if you're unsure.
Wrap-up — What to do next
  1. Open the Company Portal from the taskbar next to Outlook (Mail).
  2. Go to Apps, read each app's description, then Install.
  3. Follow any special steps in the app description for apps like 3CX and Adobe.
  4. Contact IT if an app requires approval or installation fails.
